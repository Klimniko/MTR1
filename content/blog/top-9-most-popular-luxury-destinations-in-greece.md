For there are ample luxury five-star hotels with jacuzzis and private pools. Look for them at Fira, Firostefani, Imerovigli, Oia, Pyrgos and Akrotiri, and have a blast true Zeus style.

Extra luxurious hotels can be found in Hora, Psarou, Ornos, Elia and Agios Stefanos. So, spend the dollars and enjoy the lavish life of the Greek emperors.

Do visit for a memorable luxury holiday in the crystalline waters of Greece.

A luxury holiday spot since the Roman empire days, with emerald green beaches that stretch to infinty, and one of Greece’s most romantic towns with its atmospheric buildings, piazzas and Venetian castles.
